Fishing Highlights

About Fishing

Fishing is a relaxing outdoor activity where people catch fish using rods, reels, bait, and patience. It can be done in lakes, rivers, oceans, and ponds.

Many people enjoy fishing because it is peaceful, fun, and a great way to spend time with friends and family.

Fishing Tips

  • Go fishing early in the morning or near sunset.
  • Use the right bait for the fish you want to catch.
  • Stay quiet so you do not scare the fish away.
  • Check the weather before going fishing.
  • Be patient. Fishing takes time.

Fishes Caught

Pomfret

Pomfret is a tasty saltwater fish.

Pomfret has soft meat and a mild flavour. It is often caught in coastal waters.

Grouper

Grouper is a strong reef fish.

Grouper is usually found near rocks and reefs. It is known for its firm texture.

Sea Bass

Sea bass is a popular fish to catch.

Sea bass is common in coastal waters. It is known for its clean taste and firm meat.
